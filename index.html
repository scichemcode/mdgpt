MDGPT Response Preview

Question: How do NVT and NPT ensembles differ for beginners?

Response: NVT fixes particle count, volume, and temperature, while NPT allows the volume to fluctuate to match a target pressure. Start with NVT for equilibration and move to NPT when density matters, citing foundational MD references.

Beginner checkpoints
